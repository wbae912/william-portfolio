About Me

Hi, I'm William. I'm a full-stack developer who loves to code!

I began my professional career as a financial consultant. Yet, there was something I still yearned for. I’ve always enjoyed exercising my creativity, and thus, sought an outlet that would allow me to do so.

Cue, coding! I see it as the perfect marriage of creativity and logic. It’s fascinating how you can turn ideas into tangible products with an analytical mind. I felt that with my finance background, it would help ease the transition into coding since I could apply my problem-solving and teaming skills I’ve developed in my former profession. To me, coding provides a strong sense of fulfillment and excitement. It’s a wonderful feeling when finally getting a piece of your code to work!

Outside of coding, you can often find me playing games (MMOs, RPGs, or a combination of both), playing pick-up basketball, or finding new, delicious restaurants to enjoy with my friends.

Doubletake

Client Repo | Server Repo | Demo

Doubletake is a React application that serves as an item manager. Users are able to create lists and add items to each, respectively. As users are packing their belongings, they are able to check off each item to ensure they don't leave anything behind. This application also includes helpful features such as a search bar, sort options, and ability to edit, delete, or create new lists & items.

Wortjäger (Word Hunter)

Client Repo | Server Repo | Demo

Wortjäger is a React application that teaches users simple German words via spaced repetition. German words are displayed on the page and users are expected to provide the correct English translation of the word. Spaced repetition works by more frequently showing words that users get wrong and less frequently displaying words that users answer correctly. Test your German today!

Technologies Used:

  • React.js
  • Node.js
  • Express
  • PostgreSQL

TV Tracker

Client Repo | Server Repo | Demo

TV Tracker is a React application that provides users a convenient platform to track and categorize their shows. Users are able to submit TV show entries and categorize them into lists including, "Plan to Watch", "Currently Watching", and "Completed".
